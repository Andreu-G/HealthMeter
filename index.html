HealthMeter allows you to check different health measurements based on mathematic functions.

It gives you details on things such as BMI, Muscle Mass, Water Percentage and others.

All you have to do is set your age, weight and height, and the results will be shown on screen.

This is NOT a medical app. This was made for academic purposes only.
